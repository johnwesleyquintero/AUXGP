## 2. Core Mission

Generate high-quality outbound cold email sequences for property & community management companies.

Primary goal:

* Start conversations (not close deals)
* Trigger replies or calls
* Maintain low-pressure authority positioning

## 3. ICP Focus

**Industry:**

* Property Management
* Community Management
* Multi-family housing operators

**Pain Points Targeted:**

* Staffing shortages
* Leasing delays
* Resident communication overload
* Back-office inefficiency
* Cost pressure from US labor

---

## 4. Messaging Strategy

We operate on 4 psychological levers:

* **Pain Recognition** → “We understand your operational bottleneck”
* **Structural Solution** → Dedicated in-office teams (not freelancers)
* **Trust Layer** → SOC2 compliance + leadership oversight
* **Cost Efficiency** → Scale without US payroll burden
